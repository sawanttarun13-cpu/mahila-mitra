The Day Everything Started

It was a regular college day when Vaishnavi walked over and asked for help — her PC wasn't working in the CAED Lab. She was wearing a dark blue top that day.

What started as a simple technical request quietly turned into one of the most meaningful friendships of my life. The connection felt unusual, almost as if we already knew each other from some other time.

It felt less like meeting someone for the first time, and more like reconnecting with someone I had known for a very long time.

Sometimes I wonder how a simple moment in the CAED lab in October 2024 turned into one of the most meaningful friendships in my life.

Meeting you never felt like meeting a stranger. It felt like reconnecting with someone I had known for a very long time.
